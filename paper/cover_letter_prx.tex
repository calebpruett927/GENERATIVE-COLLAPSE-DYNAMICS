\opening{Dear Editors,}

I am writing to submit the manuscript entitled \textbf{``Cross-Scale
Integrity Diagnostics: Algebraic Invariants and Universal
Phase-Transition Signatures Spanning 61~Orders of Magnitude''} for
consideration as a Research Article in \emph{Physical Review~X}.

\textbf{Summary.} We present a six-output algebraic kernel that maps
any bounded measurement vector to fidelity (weighted arithmetic
mean), composite integrity (weighted geometric mean), and four
related invariants. The ratio IC/$F$---geometric to arithmetic
coherence---exhibits structurally identical phase-transition
signatures across 406~physical objects spanning 61~orders of
magnitude. At the quark--hadron boundary, IC/$F$ collapses
$23\times$ through a ``geometric slaughter'' mechanism: a single
dead measurement channel (colour confinement driving the colour
charge to the guard band $\varepsilon = 10^{-8}$) catastrophically
suppresses the geometric mean while the arithmetic mean degrades
only linearly. Atomic structure subsequently restores coherence
$35\times$ through emergent degrees of freedom. The same mechanism
operates at the cosmological scale ($10^{26}$\,m), where dark
energy acts as the dead channel.

\textbf{Why PRX.} This work combines three elements that match
the scope of \emph{Physical Review~X}:

\begin{enumerate}
\item \textbf{Computational rigour.} 11,342~deterministic automated
  proofs across 20~scientific domains verify three algebraic
  identities to machine precision ($<10^{-15}$) with zero
  violations. Seam composition is associative to $5.55 \times
  10^{-17}$, forming an exact monoid. The entire verification
  infrastructure (246~closure modules, 47~proven lemmas, 44~structural
  identities) is open-source and reproducible.

\item \textbf{Algebraic framework.} The kernel has three effective
  degrees of freedom ($F$, $\kappa$, $C$), with entropy
  asymptotically determined by fidelity and curvature. The integrity
  bound IC~$\leq\,F$---which instantiates the classical weighted
  AM-GM inequality---acquires a solvability interpretation
  (trace recovery requires IC~$\leq\,F$ for real solutions) and
  composition laws (IC geometric, $F$ arithmetic) absent from
  the classical statement. The heterogeneity gap
  $\Delta = F - \text{IC}$ composes with a Hellinger-like
  correction term.

\item \textbf{Cross-domain scope.} The same kernel with identical
  frozen parameters produces phase-transition diagnostics across
  particle physics (31~Standard Model particles), atomic physics
  (all 118~elements), neuroscience (20~consciousness systems),
  cosmology (dark energy epochs), and 16~additional domains. Three
  testable predictions are offered: a confinement signature in
  lattice QCD, noble-gas coherence minima in multi-channel atomic
  analyses, and an adolescent geometric-coherence peak in
  neuroimaging data.
\end{enumerate}
